Creating web graphics

This is an example of a basic interactive graphic: some balls, some walls, and some falls collisions:

You control the biggest ball, and you can shoot it in the direction of wherever you click/tap in the graphic. The further away from the ball you click/tap, the faster it'll go.

You may also notice that the balls bounce against walls "at their centers" rather than at their edges, and sometimes, if things are moving real fast, balls seem to be able to just "go through each other"...???
